Travel Stories

Fall for the falls.

In the province of Bukidnon, there are numerous stunning waterfalls, some of which are still inaccessible. Fortunately, three of these waterfalls are located within CEDAR and can be easily accessed with the help of their skilled tour guides. The nearest one is Natigbasan Falls, characterized by its rocky terrain. Next is Gantungan Falls, known for its curtain-like cascades and emerald pool. The farthest is Dila Falls, which is also the highest among the three.
